Lavender Honey Crepe Cake

Adapted from here: Crepe Recipe

7" Cake
Prep time: 20 minutes Cook time: 25-30 minutes Assembly time: 20 minutes
Total: 1.5 hour

Ingredients

Crepe Batter:
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 2 tbsp honey
Lavender-Honey Filling:
  • 2 pints whipped cream
  • 1-2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tsp lavender

Directions

Crepes

Mix crepe batter ingredients until smooth.

Oil pan and heat pan on medium to high heat.

Pour 1/2 cup batter onto pan and swirl around until batter is spread thin.

Once you can see bubbles, flip crepe.

After 30 or so seconds, remove crepe from pan and let cool.

Repeat above steps until you run out of batter. This recipe yields 15-20 crepes.

Filling

Chop lavender into small pieces.

Toast lavender in a pan on low heat for 5 minutes and let cool.

Whip cream and honey together for 3-5 minutes on medium to high speeds until peaks begin to form.

Fold in lavender into whipped cream.

Cake Assembly

On a cake plate or other flat surface, lay down first crepe.

Apply about 1/2 cup of the whipped cream on top of crepe and spread around until the surface is covered. It's okay if it falls off the side.

Repeat previous step(s) until you reach your desired height for the cake.

Top with whipped cream and garnish with lavender, drizzles of honey, or fresh fruit.
